Rehabilitation Research and Training Center on VR Practices and Youth

The Center conducts research and provides trainings and technical assistance in order to improve VR service delivery to accomplish the long-term goal of improving the lives of transitioning youth with disabilities and their families.

This website will highlight the Center’s research findings, publications, related products, tools, and other resources – providing up-to-date, evidence-based information to educators, rehabilitation professionals, youth with disabilities and their families, advocates, researchers, and policymakers.
