VOCATIONAL REHABILITATION (VR) PRACTICES

The Center is charged with increasing knowledge of effective VR practices that builds on the evidence base of effective practices in transition by investigating problems, crafting solutions and designing actionable strategies that improve transition and employment outcomes for youth with disabilities. Studies in this topic area will strengthen the evidence base for effective VR programs and services via data analytics and identify common transition practices of highly effective VR counselors.

Center Publications

Find publications such as journal articles, Fast Facts, manuscripts and archived presentations generated by the Center researcher’s and partners that address VR, transition models, and improving employment outcomes for youth with disabilities.
